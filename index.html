About

Hello, my name is Cole Dorazio, I love tech, design, and the art of capturing movement. I was born and raised in Charlote, NC.

I tell real stories through creative visuals and am always learning new ways to make my ideas move. Be sure to check out my gallery.

Drones, photography, videography, and editing are among the list of services that I offer.
